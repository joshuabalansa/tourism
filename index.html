Festivals and Events

Masskara Festival

The Masskara Festival is a vibrant and lively celebration held annually in Bacolod City, Philippines. Known for its colorful masks, street dancing, and exuberant atmosphere, the festival symbolizes the resilience and optimism of the local community in the face of challenges, making it a captivating and joyful event that draws visitors from around the world.
When: A month-long celebration annually every October with highlights held every 4th sunday.
Where: Masskara Festival is celebrated in many places of Bacolod City such as Bacolod City Government Center (BCGC), Upper East Megaworld, Bacolod Public Plaza, Old Airport, and Lacson Streets.
Fb page/website: Bacolod MassKara Festival
